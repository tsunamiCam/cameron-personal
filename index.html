About Me

My expertise is in coastal modelling and analysing the risks associated with natural hazards. I have extensive experience developing hydrodynamic models, particularly of coastal hazards, as well as the technical skills necessary to distil large datasets to make sense of their relevance and practical significance.

My skill-base was developed first as an engineer, where I used numerical models of waves, storm surge, tides, and tsunamis to determine the hazard exposure of coastal and marine structures (see Triton Consultants Ltd.).

With my unique blend of scientific and technical engineering skills, I am well-positioned to inform complex decisions regarding insurance, engineering and emergency planning.
